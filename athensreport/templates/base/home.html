Follow the trolley bus route

The visual spine of the project unfolds along the route of the popular trolley line number 11, which runs through the historical center of Athens and connects two densely populated neighborhoods Pagrati and Patissia. The video, shot by cameras mounted on the trolley, provides the main navigation and creates a topographical structure for the topical and chronological audiovisual archives that are hyperlinked from it.
Trolley 11 was selected as the video path through these tumultuous years because of the emblematic significance of the neighborhoods it transverves: areas of thousands of undocumented immigrants, middle class Greek districts, and the center of the city of Athens with the Greek Parliament, luxury hotels, banks and the university.
