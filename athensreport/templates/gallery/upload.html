Upload Media

"Athens Report" seeks your participation to build a collective bottom-up project which, by filing personal experiences in video format, will contribute to re-representation of the years of recession and austerity in Athens against the dominant representation through the traditional media.

Upload your videos in any format and quality. The material should record events that occurred on the axis of the route of the trolley # 11.

The curatorial team of "Athens Report" will check your material and archive them. The curatorial team has the right to edit the original material for their duration but will not modify them.
Depending on the volume, uploaded media might take up to six weeks to be posted.

To upload photos, please contact us at: info@athensreport.org
With the following information: Location of the photo, date of the event, and description if available.

To upload graffiti photos, fill in the fields: Location and Date of event in the video form.
